Hot dog

The American hot dog. The quintessential American cuisine and purveyor of all that is good in this world. We must admit: These aren't fancy. But it doesn't matter; nothing slaps like a good ol fashioned American hot dog.

Preparation is simple and straightforward, so you'll be bun-to-mouth in no time.

Ingredients

  • Bun
  • Hot dog link of choice
  • Ketchup, mustard, or other condiment

Steps

  1. Boil the desired amount of hot dogs for approximately 5 minutes.
  2. Remove one hot dog sausage from the water and place in an open bun.
  3. Add condiments as desired.
  4. Enjoy!
