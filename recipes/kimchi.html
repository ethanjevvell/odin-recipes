Kimchi

Description

Kimchi is the de facto national dish of the two Koreas. Before modern refridgeration, the dish was prepared by burying cabbage in large vase-like containers for extended periods of time to let it ferment.

After several months, women would dig up the vases and process the kimchi.

Ingredients

  • Cabbage
  • Sprite
  • Vase

Steps

  1. Dig a hole during early winter.
  2. Wash and prepare the cabbage.
  3. Let the cabbage ferment for approximately six months.
  4. Extract the kimchi and enjoy!
